Target Audience

This site is for anyone that is trying to grow their business or seek out and make connections. This can be done by learning about Provo and how to better suit your business to the needs of the people here.

Scenarios

  • What does the Chamber of Commerce do?
  • How do I get involved?
  • I am new to the area and want to know more.
  • The advantages and disadvantages of the chamber.
  • What businesses are already involved?

Persona 1

After developing a successful company in California, these executives are looking to expand their company. They are looking for all the information they can gather about Provo and how it might benefit them to expand there. They can look at the businesses that are a part of the Chamber and contact them to find out even more about the market.

Persona 2

This young woman is looking to start her own business. She knows about Provo because she has lived there her whole life. She is looking to see what the chamber of commerce could do for her to help grow her business. It would be beneficial for her to make connections with suppliers and businesses in her homewtown and this would be a great starting point.
