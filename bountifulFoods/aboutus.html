About Us

Our history

Our founder Joe Carlson started with just a truck and a dream. He would drive around everywhere around Carlsbad California looking for interested customers. Being mobile was important because our presence could be felt a lot more. Just like the local Ice cream truck Joe pushed forward through every neighborhood.

Bountiful foods started with a dream. To provide healthy organic fruit based foods for everyone. We also wanted to be able to provide people iwth information about what they are eating. That includes the nutrition facts and even who grew that fruit. We think this provides better transparency, so we can build trust with our customers.

Today Bountiful foods consists of four food trucks and a physical location in Carlsbad California. We look forward to expanding more and more so we can provide tasty drinks for all!

Where We Are Located

We are located in sunny Carlsbad California. Enjoy the views of the ocean while you sip on a fresh made fruit drink. Soak up the sun and cool down with a fresh nutritious beverage.

Our food trucks have gone as far north as Irvine and as south as San Diego. Make sure to checkout our social media so you can know where we're at!
